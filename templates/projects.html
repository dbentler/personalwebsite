The LW Network

Snippet of Code from LW-Core

I know know what you may be thinking: "A minecraft server on your portfolio"? And... yeah it is. But I didn't put it up for just any old reason.

The LW-Network was a business started by me and three friends. From June 2019 - December 2019, we amassed a community of hundreds of members, and I was in charge of our servers and local development. It was through the LW-Network that I learned about Git and version control, and had the opportunity to develop features requested by both our volunteer team, and players.

I was also in charge of managing our servers, hosted by OVH, and learned a great deal about Linux and server management.

At the end of our run, we had generated $21,000 USD in revenue and exceeded the Discord server member limit (1,000 back in 2019).

For a business hosted in a video game by a 19 year old at the time, I think that's pretty impressive.
